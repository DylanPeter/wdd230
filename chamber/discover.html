Statistical Data

  • In 2022, the Greater Las Vegas Chamber of Commerce's revenue was $5.97 million, expenses were $5.55 million, and total assets were $10 million.
  • In 2022, the Greater Las Vegas Chamber of Commerce's total liabilities were $2.41 million.
  • Tax returns filed by nonprofit organizations are public records.
  • The Vegas Chamber is the largest business support organization in Nevada.
  • The Vegas Chamber's focus is on helping Nevada businesses succeed and create jobs.
