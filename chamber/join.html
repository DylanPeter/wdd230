Membership Information

Bronze Membership

Monthly fee: $50

    Benefits:
  • Listing in chamber directory
  • Access to chamber networking events
  • Basic marketing support

    • Silver Membership

    Monthly fee: $100

      All bronze benefits, plus:
  • Priority listing in chamber directory
  • Additional marketing opportunities
  • Access to exclusive chamber workshops

    • Gold Membership

    Monthly fee: $200

      All silver benefits, plus:
  • Featured listing in chamber directory
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Sponsorship opportunities at chamber events
    • NP Membership (Non-Profit)

    No monthly fee

      Benefits:
  • Access to chamber networking events
  • Listing in chamber directory as non-profit
  • Basic marketing support tailored for non-profits
