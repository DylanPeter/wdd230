3 Design Priciples that I wanted to learn about

Netflix

This is a poor demonstration of Hick's Law. Hick's Law explains that when someone is presented with too many options to choose something, they will get overwhelmed. The reason why it takes so long to choose a show or movie on Netfix is because there are too many options to pick from.

Apple

This is a great visual demonstration of the idea a Visual Hierarchy because it is clearly structured to show their different products in a clean orderly way. It shows off the most important and new products in a list format, and then it shows some of the less popular items in a block format.

The Avengers Movie

In this image from the Thor movie, the frame perfctly aligns with the rule of thirds grid that draws the viewer into the movie and allows them to imagine what could happen next.
